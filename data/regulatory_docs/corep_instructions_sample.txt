# COREP Reporting Instructions - Own Funds (C 01.00)

## Template C 01.00 - Own Funds

### Purpose
This template collects information on the composition of own funds of institutions, broken down by Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1), Additional Tier 1 (AT1), and Tier 2 capital.

### Reporting Frequency
Quarterly

### Template Structure

#### Section 1: Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) Capital - Instruments and Reserves

**Row 010 - Capital instruments and the related share premium accounts**
Report the amount of capital instruments eligible as CET1 capital and their related share premium accounts.
Instruction: Include only instruments that meet all criteria specified in Article 28 of CRR.

**Row 020 - Retained earnings**
Report retained earnings including interim or year-end profits verified by persons responsible for the auditing of the accounts.
Instruction: Include only verified profits. Exclude any foreseeable charges or dividends.

**Row 030 - Accumulated other comprehensive income (and other reserves)**
Report the amount of accumulated other comprehensive income and other disclosed reserves.
Instruction: Include reserves required by national law, statutory reserves, and other disclosed reserves.

**Row 040 - Funds for general banking risk**
Report funds for general banking risk recognized in equity under applicable accounting standards.

**Row 050 - Minority interests (amount allowed in consolidated CET1)**
Report the amount of minority interests that can be included in consolidated CET1 capital.

**Row 060 - Independently reviewed interim profits net of any foreseeable charge or dividend**
Report interim profits that have been independently reviewed and are net of foreseeable charges.

#### Section 2: Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) Capital - Regulatory Adjustments

**Row 070 - Intangible assets (net of related tax liability)**
Report the value of intangible assets including goodwill, net of any associated deferred tax liability.
Instruction: This is a DEDUCTION from CET1 capital. Report as a positive number.

**Row 080 - Deferred tax assets that rely on future profitability**
Report deferred tax assets that depend on future profitability excluding those arising from temporary differences.
Instruction: This is a DEDUCTION from CET1 capital. Report as a positive number.

**Row 090 - Negative amounts resulting from the calculation of expected loss amounts**
Report any negative amounts resulting from expected loss calculations under the IRB approach.
Instruction: This is a DEDUCTION from CET1 capital. Report as a positive number.

**Row 100 - Direct and indirect holdings of own CET1 instruments**
Report direct, indirect, and synthetic holdings of the institution's own CET1 instruments.
Instruction: This is a DEDUCTION from CET1 capital. Report as a positive number.

**Row 110 - Total regulatory adjustments to CET1**
Sum of all regulatory adjustments (deductions) from CET1 capital.
Calculation: Sum of rows 070 to 100.

**Row 120 - Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) capital**
Total CET1 capital after regulatory adjustments.
Calculation: (Sum of rows 010 to 060) - Row 110.

#### Section 3: Additional Tier 1 (AT1) Capital

**Row 130 - Capital instruments and the related share premium accounts**
Report AT1 capital instruments and related share premium accounts.
Instruction: Include only instruments meeting all criteria for AT1 classification.

**Row 140 - Instruments issued by subsidiaries (amount allowed in consolidated AT1)**
Report AT1 instruments issued by subsidiaries that qualify for inclusion in consolidated AT1 capital.

**Row 150 - Total regulatory adjustments to AT1 capital**
Report total deductions from AT1 capital including holdings of own AT1 instruments.

**Row 160 - Additional Tier 1 (AT1) capital**
Total AT1 capital after regulatory adjustments.
Calculation: (Row 130 + Row 140) - Row 150.

**Row 170 - Tier 1 capital (T1 = CET1 + AT1)**
Total Tier 1 capital.
Calculation: Row 120 + Row 160.

#### Section 4: Tier 2 (T2) Capital

**Row 180 - Capital instruments and the related share premium accounts**
Report Tier 2 capital instruments and subordinated loans with related share premium.
Instruction: Include only instruments meeting Tier 2 criteria with minimum 5-year maturity.

**Row 190 - Instruments issued by subsidiaries (amount allowed in consolidated T2)**
Report Tier 2 instruments issued by subsidiaries qualifying for consolidated Tier 2 capital.

**Row 200 - Credit risk adjustments**
Report general credit risk adjustments for institutions using the IRB approach.
Instruction: Limited to 1.25% of risk-weighted exposure amounts.

**Row 210 - Total regulatory adjustments to T2 capital**
Report total deductions from Tier 2 capital.

**Row 220 - Tier 2 (T2) capital**
Total Tier 2 capital after regulatory adjustments.
Calculation: (Row 180 + Row 190 + Row 200) - Row 210.

**Row 230 - Total capital (TC = T1 + T2)**
Total own funds.
Calculation: Row 170 + Row 220.

### Validation Rules

1. Row 120 (CET1) must be positive
2. Row 170 (Tier 1) must be >= Row 120 (CET1)
3. Row 230 (Total Capital) must be >= Row 170 (Tier 1)
4. All deduction rows (070-100, 150, 210) must be reported as positive numbers
5. Interim profits (Row 060) require independent verification
